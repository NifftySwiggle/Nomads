FAQ

Questions from the convoy

Short answers, no fluff. If you need deeper docs, we’ll link them once mint is live and contracts are deployed.
How do I get a Rare trait?
Rare traits live inside the main Nomads collection. You mint like everyone else — if RNG smiles, your Nomad reveals with rare traits.
Is there a whitelist or presale?
No. Mint is public by design. Early allies are rewarded through airdrops, reserved supply, and future experiments instead of private pricing games.
Where can I see secondary listings?
View Nomad Dogs on Magic Eden or OpenSea. Always verify the contract address (0xbD11...49c1) matches before buying.
How does staking work?
Staking is non-custodial through the Gaia Ecosystem platform. You keep full ownership of your NFTs while earning rewards. No locking contracts — you can unstake anytime.
What's the connection to Pitbits?
Pitbits is the original collection on Cronos. Nomad Dogs evolved from that universe onto Monad. Both collections coexist — neither replaces the other. Pitbits holders get reserved Nomads.
When will NFTs be revealed?
NFTs reveal after sellout or if market conditions change.
What wallet do I need?
Any Monad-compatible wallet works. MetaMask, Rabby, or similar. Make sure to add Monad network (Chain ID 143) and have $MON for minting and gas fees.
What's the vision after mint?
Use Nomads as a modular base to explore the Monad ecosystem: staking, collaborations, visual upgrades, and trait‑driven experiments.
How do I stay updated?
Follow our official X account and join the Discord. Mint progress, and future drops will be announced from there.
Can I mint multiple Nomads?
Yes, up to 20 per wallet. Batch minting is supported to save on gas fees if you want multiple Nomads in one transaction.
Will there be future utility?
Absolutely. Beyond staking rewards, holders get access to future airdrops, exclusive tools on nifftyswiggle.pw, and early access to upcoming projects in the Monad ecosystem.
What are the 999 reserved NFTs for?
Reserved supply goes to Pitbits holders (who supported the original collection), team allocations, marketing partnerships, and future community rewards. these unlock gradually.
Is the smart contract audited?
The contract is built on NFTs2Me's audited ERC721A standard, which is battle-tested across multiple chains. It's optimized for gas efficiency and security.
